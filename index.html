IEEE INDIA:


IEEE India council is the umbrella organization which coordinates all IEEE activities in India. It primarily aims at assisting and coordinating the activities of the local “sections”, in order to benefit mutually, and avoid duplication of the efforts and resources.
IEEE India was established on 20 May 1976 and is one among the five councils in the Asia Pacific Region.

History of IEEE:


The roots of formation of IEEE comes from the amalgamation of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers and the Institute of Radio Engineers in the year 1963.Its corporate office is at New York and its operations center is in Piscataway, New Jersey.
In 1884, the American Institute of Electrical Engineers was formed. And in 1912, the rival Institution of Radio Engineers was formed. Although AIEE was initially large, later IRE attracted large number of students.


Growth of IEEE:


The two institutions, AIEE and IRE merged to form IEEE on 1st January 1963. At that time, the group had 150,000 members. By 1984, there were 250,000 members. Today IEEE has over 500,000 members in about 160 countries across the world.
With its tagline being “ ADVANCING TECHNOLOGY FOR HUMANITY”.

STUDENT BRANCH : In the 1985, the student branch at GIT emerged with just 4-5 active members of the IEEE. Later it grew large to become the Active Student Branch in North Karnataka , and as help numerous Student Branch to grow big.
Earlier the Student Branch was just publishing only the technical papers, later as it grew it started paving way to many student activities and student technical
The Student Branch aims at becoming the best Student Branch and the best BC. It gives assurance of giving the students the best of facilities for the enhancement of the required techino-managerial skills. The outstanding events held will give the quality certification for the skills an individual posess.
The student branch focuses on conducting social and technical activities for students and encourage them to take advantage of the IEEE membership which includes the participation in events,conferences,competition and many more.
PAANCHAJANYA:
Paanchajanya is a annual flagship organized by the Student Branch at GIT. With its motto being “EDUTAINMENT” which means education with entertainment which will be the students enhance their Techno-Managerial skills. According to the Bhagvat Gita, Paanchajanya is the conchshell of the Krishna avatar of Lord Vishnu. This conch and its sound symbolizes the victory of dharma over adharma, superiority of light over darkness.
This annual event was named as Paanchajanya by a student Mr.Anurag Agarwal and his team. Paanchajanya was first held in the year 2006, then onwards this event is held every year as a annual fellowship and has gained popularity among students of various Engineering Institutes of the region.

Events of IEEE

PAANCHAJANYA :

The biggest annual event of the IEEE GIT was started in the year 2006, and is organized every year by the IEEE to promote the technical skills development among the students.
The idea of paanchajanya is betterment of techno-managerial skills of the participates. With its motto –“EDUTAINMENT”. The word paanchajanya means the conch of Lord Krishna which is the symbol of the victory of knowledge over ignorance.
This annual flagship event became very popular not only among the students of GIT but also among the students of various other institutes of the region. This is a regional level inter-college technical symposium for students.
The competitions include idea presentation, projects etc. the idea presentations and few BOT projects have been invited at the Incubation center of the college.

ACHIEVEMENTS OFIEEE

  • Establishing IEEE in GIT

    Our faculty from the department of E&C Engg. And Computer Science Engg came together to form a group to access and submit technical papers in IEEE. This group then took membership in this professional body in 1980’s.

    1980

  • GITCON in GIT

    The GITCON was organized in the 1990’ss to provide the researcher’s faculties and students a common platform to showcase their technical skills. This initiated activities under the Technical Chapter of IEEE in the academic year 1995-96.

    1994-95

  • The official annoucement of Student branch formation by MGA

    The formation of a professional Student Branch at KLS’s GIT in the year 2003 was officially announced by the MGA(Member and Geographic Activities) under IEEE.KLS’s Gogte Institute of Technology’s Student Branch got certified on 17th June 2003. This was the first professional chapter of the Institute.the Student Branch has been actively contributing to the IEEE community and has been serving in the professional community.

    2003

  • Grant of 300$ from IEEE headquaters

    The Student Branch received a grant of 300$ from IEEE headquarters under the STAR program. This event was dedicated to the rural and urban girls to take up interest in science and technology.

    2014-15

  • Compeletion of 3 Decades of IEEE at GIT

    In the year 2015, the Student Branch celebrated 3 decades of association with IEEE, and 2 decades of holding the first technical conference in the institute,and also a decade of conducting the annual flagship event of IEEE GIT the paanchajanya which is a popular event among various institutes of the region. This event was marked special with the felicitation of the pioneer of the institute who initiated IEEE professional activities: Dr.D H Rao ;Shri. Hemant Kumar;Dr. R V Kulkarni; Dr G R Udupi and Prof. Vineeta P Gejjji

    2015

  • IEEE GIT hosts NKSYWC

    In 2016, the Student Branch hosted the first North Karnataka Student Young Professional and WIE Congress(NKSYWC).

    2016
